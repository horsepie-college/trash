Aztec Ruins Puzzle!

Welcome to our latest project, Aztec Ruins Puzzle! We're excited to share this new addition to our collection of games with you all.

Aztec Ruins Puzzle is a challenging yet fun pixel art puzzle game that takes place deep within ancient Aztec temples. As players navigate through the ruins, they will encounter various obstacles and riddles that must be solved to progress further into the temple.

The game features beautifully crafted pixel art graphics that bring the ancient world of the Aztecs to life on your iPhone 5 or Android device. The simple controls make it easy for anyone to pick up and play, but don't let the simplicity fool you - the puzzles get increasingly difficult as you move forward.

One of the things we are most proud of is the fact that Aztec Ruins Puzzle is completely cross-platform compatible, meaning you can seamlessly switch between playing on your phone and tablet without missing a beat. This is made possible by using Java as the programming language, which allows us to easily port the game to multiple platforms.

We hope you enjoy exploring the mysterious depths of the Aztec Temple in Aztec Ruins Puzzle. Thank you for supporting independent developers like ourselves, and happy gaming!

"Jump 'n Run"! First released back in 2013, this classic platformer has since become a fan favorite for many. The game offers engaging gameplay and vibrant visuals that appeal to gamers of all ages.

In Jump 'n Run, players take control of a character who must navigate through various levels filled with obstacles and traps. To advance, they must jump over pits, avoid enemies, and collect valuable items scattered throughout the stages. With multiple characters to choose from, each with their own unique skills and abilities, there is plenty of replay value for players looking to perfect their runs.

Additionally, Jump 'n Run includes exciting powerups and special items that allow players to unlock secret paths and gain powerful boosts. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just starting out, there is something here for everyone to enjoy.

If you haven't already experienced Jump 'n Run, now is the time to check it out! Its enduring popularity speaks volumes about the quality of the game itself. And who knows? You might even find yourself getting lost in its addictive gameplay for hours at a time. Stay tuned for updates on our latest projects and thanks for being part of our community!
