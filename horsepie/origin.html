The origins of Silas!

Many years ago, in a small village nestled at the foot of a great mountain range, there lived an ordinary man named Silas. He was known by his neighbors to be quite silly and prone to fits of laughter for no apparent reason.

One day, as he was wandering through the mountains on one of his many adventures, he stumbled upon an ancient temple hidden away in a remote valley. Curious about what lay inside, he ventured forth into the darkened interior of the temple, where he discovered a mysterious artifact - a column decorated with strange symbols.

As soon as he laid eyes on it, Silas felt a sudden surge of energy course through him. With a booming voice, the columns spoke to him, telling him that they were searching for someone who could take their power and use it for good.

Though he had never been considered particularly intelligent or wise, Silas knew immediately that this was his destiny. He took hold of the first column and suddenly found himself transformed into the silly man we know today from the Columns 3 game cover!

From then on, whenever danger threatened the land, the people would call upon Silas and watch as he whirled his columns around, creating spectacular patterns of color and light that vanquished evil wherever it dared appear. And even now, when he's not busy saving the world, you can find him laughing heartily somewhere, because after all, being a hero is just another excuse to have fun!
